Biryani, a dish that needs no introduction in the culinary world, is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish that has its roots deeply embedded in the rich tapestry of Indian cuisine. Among the myriad variations of biryani, one that stands out for its robust taste and hearty flavors is beef biryani. This dish is a celebration of tender beef pieces marinated in a blend of spices, layered with aromatic rice, and slow-cooked to perfection.
